why collect

"Collecting isn't about the object. It's about owning a piece of something you felt once — and refusing to let it become just a memory."

The cards, the autos, the jersey patches — they're not investments. They're receipts. Proof that a moment mattered enough to hold onto. If you grew up with Gretzky on TV, with Hawkeye in the comics, with the feeling that some things deserved to last — you already understand this. Nobody else has to.
